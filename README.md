# Job Search Companion

A free, guided workbook for finding your next job by looking for companies you actually want to work for, instead of scrolling through job boards. Based on the Job Search Strategy workshop by [Dominika Rogala · Good Job Coffee](https://goodjob.coffee).

**Use it here: https://tinwene.github.io/job-search-companion/**

Everything you type stays in your browser (localStorage). Nothing is uploaded anywhere. Use the built-in "Save backup" button to keep a copy of your work.

This is an early version. Feedback: dominika@goodjob.coffee
